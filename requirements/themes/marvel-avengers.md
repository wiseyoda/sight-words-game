## Overview

**Full Title**: Marvel Avengers: Word Warriors
**Target Age**: 5-7 years
**Difficulty**: Advanced
**Campaign Length**: 15 missions + 1 boss

---

## Story Synopsis

> Thanos has scattered the Infinity Words across the universe!
> Without them, the Avengers can't communicate their super moves!
> Assemble Earth's mightiest heroes and rebuild sentences to save the universe!

### Story Arc

| Act | Missions | Plot |
|-----|----------|------|
| **Act 1** | 1-5 | Recruit the Avengers |
| **Act 2** | 6-10 | Gather the Infinity Words |
| **Act 3** | 11-15 | Battle Thanos's minions |
| **Boss** | 16 | Final confrontation with Thanos |

## Special Marvel Elements

### Power-Up Effects

Each hero has a signature power effect when their missions complete:
- **Iron Man**: Repulsor blast particle effect
- **Spider-Man**: Web shooting across screen
- **Hulk**: Screen shake
- **Thor**: Lightning flash
- **Black Panther**: Vibranium wave

### Health Bar (Boss Only)

The boss mission uniquely features a "Thanos Health Bar":
- Starts at 100%
- Decreases ~14% per correct sentence
- Visual: Purple bar turning gold
- At 0%: Explosion, victory

### Difficulty Note

This theme is intentionally harder:
- More missions (15 + boss vs 12 + boss)
- Longer sentences
- Earlier scaffolding reduction
- Recommended after completing another theme
