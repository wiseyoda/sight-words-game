## Overview

**Full Title**: Bluey: Backyard Adventures
**Target Age**: 4-7 years
**Difficulty**: Mid-range
**Campaign Length**: 12 missions + 1 boss

---

## Story Synopsis

> Bluey and Bingo discovered a magical game where building sentences unlocks amazing backyard adventures!
> Each word you place opens a new game to play with the whole family.
> Let's play!

### Story Arc

| Act | Missions | Plot |
|-----|----------|------|
| **Act 1** | 1-4 | Backyard games with Bluey and Bingo |
| **Act 2** | 5-8 | Games with Mum and Dad join in |
| **Act 3** | 9-12 | The whole family plays together |
| **Boss** | 13 | The Ultimate Family Game Day |

## Special Bluey Elements

### "For Real Life" Moments

Occasionally, sentences relate to real life lessons:
- Sharing
- Patience
- Family love
- Being kind

### Dance Mode

When certain missions complete, a brief "dance mode" celebration plays:
- Characters dance
- Music changes to upbeat
- Confetti falls

### Emotional Beats

Some missions (like Sleepy Time) have softer, more emotional conclusions:
- Warmer music
- Slower animations
- Heartfelt voice lines
