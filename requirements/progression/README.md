## Progression Layers

### 1. Mission Progress

- Complete missions to advance on Story Map
- Each mission earns 1-3 stars
- Boss mission unlocks next campaign

### 2. Star Accumulation

- Stars are permanent (never lost)
- Total stars unlock milestones
- Displayed prominently throughout

### 3. Word Mastery

- Individual word tracking
- Four mastery levels
- Progress toward "all mastered"

### 4. Unlockables

- Avatars (profile customization)
- Stickers (collection)
- Mini-games (gameplay variety)

### 5. Campaign Completion

- Each theme has one campaign
- Boss battle at the end
- Unlocks next theme/campaign

---

## Motivation Design

### Short-Term (Within Session)

- Stars earned per mission
- Progress bar per mission
- Celebrations after each sentence

### Medium-Term (Across Sessions)

- Story Map progression
- Sticker collection
- Avatar unlocks

### Long-Term (Weeks/Months)

- Total star milestones
- Campaign completion
- Word mastery percentage

---

## Key Principle

> **"Never Punish"**
>
> Progress only moves forward. Stars are never lost.
> Replaying missions can only improve stars, never reduce.
